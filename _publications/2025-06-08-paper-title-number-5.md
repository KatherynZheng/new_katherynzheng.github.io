**Authors:**  
Zibin Pan, Zhichao Wang, Chi Li, **Kaiyan Zheng**, Boqi Wang, Xiaoying Tang, Junhua Zhao  

**Published:** April 11, 2025  

This paper studies federated unlearning under gradient-based optimization and introduces a conflict mitigation mechanism to stabilize decentralized updates. The proposed approach improves unlearning efficiency while preserving model utility in distributed learning systems.
